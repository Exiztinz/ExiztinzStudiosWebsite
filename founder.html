Who I Am

My name is Joseph Tascona and I'm the founder of Exiztinz Studios — a small indie game development studio based in Barrie, Ontario. I've been building games since I was a teenager, starting with rough prototypes in whatever engine I could get my hands on and slowly getting better at every part of it.

I founded Exiztinz Studios because I wanted to build games on my own terms — no publisher breathing down my neck, no design-by-committee, no "make it more accessible" notes from someone who hasn't played a game in a decade. Just honest, handcrafted experiences that mean something.

I work across the full development pipeline — from initial concept and systems design to level building, code architecture, and shipping. I believe in keeping teams lean, scopes honest, and quality high.
