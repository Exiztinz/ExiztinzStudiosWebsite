INDIE GAME DEVELOPMENT

Let's Build Worlds

Exiztinz Studios is a small indie team making the kinds of games we've always wanted to play. No publisher. No mandated sequels. Just handcrafted worlds built with real care and a lot of stubbornness. We believe the best games come from people who genuinely love what they're making — and we're always looking for those kinds of people to work with us.

Built from the Ground Up

Exiztinz Studios started as one person with a vision and too much free time. It grew into a small, tight-knit team of developers, artists, and storytellers who all care deeply about the craft. We're indie in every sense — independent in our funding, our decisions, and our creative direction. We move slowly, we finish things, and we make games we're genuinely proud of.

OUR GAMES

Project Phantom

Our most personal and most ambitious project. An open-world action-RPG years in the making — built slowly, deliberately, and exactly the way we want it. Every system exists because the game needs it. Every corner of the world has something real in it. This is the one we're building for keeps.

Echoes of the Fallen

A dark fantasy RPG that started as a sketch in a notebook and grew into something we couldn't put down. Explore a world that's already lost — uncover what broke it, build something that might last, or burn the rest down. The choices feel real because we wrote them to be.

LEARN MORE WATCH TRAILER

Void Protocol

Our first shipped game. Came out of a 72-hour game jam prototype we couldn't stop working on. A co-op sci-fi shooter with procedurally generated missions and an alien threat that actually keeps you guessing. It's rough in places — but it's ours, and the community that showed up for it means everything.

LEARN MORE WATCH TRAILER

Stormfront: Legends

Started as an experiment — could a tiny indie team actually ship a live competitive multiplayer game? Turns out yes, barely, and now it has ranked seasons and a community we genuinely didn't expect. A fast, tactical roster-based brawler that keeps evolving because the players keep showing up.

LOCATIONS

Barrie, Ontario, Canada

Exiztinz Studios is located in Barrie, Ontario, on the shores of Kempenfelt Bay. Barrie offers a strong mix of waterfront living, growing tech and creative business, year-round events, and quick access to outdoor adventure. From downtown food spots and live entertainment to nearby ski hills, trails, and lake activities, there is always something happening close to the studio. We believe where you live and work matters, and Barrie gives our team a balanced lifestyle with energy in every season.
