Award-Winning & Groundbreaking

OUR GAMES

Project Phantom

Project Phantom is Exiztinz Studios' most ambitious title to date — a sprawling open-world action-RPG set in a hand-crafted universe teeming with hidden secrets, dynamic factions, and a branching narrative that responds to every choice you make. No two playthroughs are ever the same.

Echoes of the Fallen

Echoes of the Fallen is a dark fantasy role-playing game that invites players to explore a fractured realm on the edge of collapse. Uncover ancient conspiracies, forge powerful alliances, and determine the fate of an entire civilization — the choices you make will echo through time.

Void Protocol

Winner of multiple industry awards, Void Protocol is Exiztinz Studios' critically acclaimed sci-fi shooter that redefined online co-operative gameplay. Team up with friends, tackle dynamic missions across procedurally generated environments, and fight back against an ever-evolving alien threat. Available now on all major platforms.
